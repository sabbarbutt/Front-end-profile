Beyond Diet

Think

Created much needed contemporary design wireframes keeping all new functionalities in mind. Sketched wireframes taking into account that the site is for a radio host who has a large number of listeners and followers on her blog page.

Design

Designed a multilayered menu and navigation to allow ease of access to the great collection of articles and program available. Redesigned and developed a PHP based website. Created wireframes that took into account that the site is for a dietician who has a large quantity of followers.

Build

Integration of new functionalities such as a URL generator, login page for users and making sure users did not face issues while browsing through the site. Implementation of design was done in such a manner that it did not hinder the upkeep of the website.
