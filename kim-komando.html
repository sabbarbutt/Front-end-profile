Kim Komando

Think

The redesign and structuring of Kim Kamando was a complex and delicate matter; since the site had a high up keep, and the client wanted many new functionalities to add to the site. I had to find a way to tackle this issue; Implantation of the restructuring while allowing the site to maintain its user inflow, with continuously added functionalities.

Design

In my attempt to improve the site’s design while adapting to new and improved functionalities added to the site constantly, I made several mock-ups to adjust the content and functionality. The Client and I finalized the design currently being used, which accommodates all the client’s needs while giving a brand new outlook to the site.

Build

Kim Komando’s site had two sections, There was a Blog and a Store section; I was responsible for building out the blog for the site. The blog was built out on WordPress; and I was able to create custom plugins to put together the functionality required by the Client.

Backend Development

New Draft URL Generation

Any blog needs to have writers, editors, and administrators. In order to create a better edited and share-worthy blog; I was tasked with creating URLs for draft posts so writers could quickly share drafts with editors and get the correct information to the Public

Backend User Accessibility to Content

Heavy Traffic on any website means layers of administrative control, in order to keep a close controlled check on the access to content I developed special functions to enhance the Super admin’s level of control over content management.

Custom Counter

Everyone loves a countdown; so for Kim Komando I set-up a site-wide countdown for every weeks show. This way users on the site waiting to tune into the show won’t miss a second.
